Noah Houpt

I'm 21 years old, and am currently attending school at Case Western Reserve University in Cleveland, OH.

I initially became fascinated with computers through my love of video games. I started with a Playstation 2 when I was 8 years old, from there I quickly transitioned over to computer gaming. When I was 12, my family's desktop couldn't handle World of Warcraft which meant I wanted a PC with better specs. After some research, my family decided that the most economical option involved assembling a PC ourselves. After purchasing the individual PC components, I built my first ever PC. This process resulted in me dipping my toes into the world of computer science and engineering, and sparked the passion I have today of becomming a software engineer.
