What I'm working on

I just finished up my time as a software engineering intern at the U.S. Census Bureau as a 2019 Civic Digital Fellow. I worked as a full-stack engineer to implement a dashboard for monitoring system usage statistics of AWS resources for the Disclosure Avoidance System (DAS). The DAS is the largest scale implementation of differential privacy ever attempted, and will be used on the 2020 decennial census to protect the privacy of all Americans. This opportunity was made possible by Coding It Forward which is a non-profit created to bring tech innovation to the federal government.
